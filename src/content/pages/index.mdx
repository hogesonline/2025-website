# PyCon AU, Australia's peak Python conference

In 2024, PyCon AU was held from the **22nd to the 26th of November** at the **Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Centre (MCEC) in Narrm/Melbourne**.

Keynotes from [**Linda McIver**](https://youtu.be/nUvvfGN-dHs?feature=shared) and [**Kendra Vant**](https://youtu.be/ddDVhbz3sNw?feature=shared) are now published to the [**PyCon AU YouTube channel**](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLs4CJRBY5F1Jn7fWZyMgogpPsu1vAZKB2) and the full program of talks continue to be uploaded this week.

Thank you to all our speakers, workshop facilitators, lightning talk presenters, volunteers and sponsors. ALL of you made this PyCon AU a fantastic conference.

Stay tuned to announcements (including about PyCon AU 2025 and beyond) by following us on:

* [Mastodon](https://mastodon.pycon.org.au/@pyconau)
* [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/pyconau/)
* or [X/Twitter](https://twitter.com/PyConAU)
