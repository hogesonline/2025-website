# Refund Policy

Email your order code to contact@pycon.org.au to request a refund.

## No questions asked refund

We will give you a no questions asked partial refund up until the following dates:

| Date | Refund amount |
| - | - |
| 15th of August, 2025 | 90% of sale price |
| 29th of August, 2025 | 50% of sale price |

## Some questions asked refund

We will give you a 100% refund in the following circumstances:
- You become eligible for a free ticket, e.g. by having your talk proposal or volunteer application accepted
- You test positive for, or display symptoms of, a communicable disease such as COVID-19
- On compassionate grounds, at the discretion of the conference director
 
 You will need to tell us which circumstance applies to you and we may ask you to supply proof.
