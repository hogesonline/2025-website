# T-shirt sizing

We will be offering two cuts of shirt: fitted and straight cut. Below are the size charts of the shirt stock we are using; they are accurate to within 2.5cm.

## Fitted

Our fitted shirts are printed on [ASColour Maple 4001](https://www.ascolour.com.au/wos-maple-tee-4001/) T-shirt stock.

| Measurement      | XS   | S    | M    | L    | XL   | 2XL  | 3XL  |
| ---------------- | ---- | ---- | ---- | ---- | ---- | ---- | ---- |
| Body width (cm)  | 45.5 | 48   | 50.5 | 53   | 55.5 | 58   | 60.5 |
| Body length (cm) | 63.5 | 64.5 | 65.5 | 66.5 | 67.5 | 68.5 | 69.5 |
